## Common Objections & Responses

**"We don't have budget for this."**  
→ "No problem. We start free—you use the platform for 30 days at no cost. If you like it, we talk pricing then. Fair?"

**"We're happy with our current system."**  
→ "I hear that. Most clinics are managing somehow. The question is: are you spending 10+ hours/week coordinating volunteers? That's the pain we solve. Worth 15 minutes to see if it's relevant?"

**"We're too small for this."**  
→ "Actually, smaller clinics get the most value—less admin overhead to begin with. Our smallest users are 15-volunteer clinics. Cost is based on size, so you'd be in the affordable tier."

**"Can you send something in writing?"**  
→ "Absolutely. Let's do a quick 15-min call first so I understand your workflow, then I'll send something tailored to you. Sound good?"
