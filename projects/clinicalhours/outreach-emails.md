## Personalization Checklist

Before sending each email:
- [ ] Find volunteer coordinator/director name (LinkedIn, call main number, clinic website)
- [ ] Replace [Name] with actual person
- [ ] Replace [Clinic Name] with actual clinic
- [ ] Add 1-2 specific details about their current system/pain (research their website, social)
- [ ] Keep signature consistent: Name, ClinicalHours, URL, phone
- [ ] Subject line should be clinic-specific (not generic)
