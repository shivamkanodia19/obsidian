## Outreach Strategy

### What to look for in each contact:
1. **Volunteer coordinator/director** name and email
2. **Current pain points:** "How do you currently manage volunteer applications?" → email/spreadsheet/forms = ClinicalHours fit
3. **Volume:** How many active volunteers? (Larger = clearer ROI)
4. **Decision maker:** Usually clinic director or volunteer coordinator
5. **Budget:** "Do you currently pay for any volunteer management tools?" → If no, easier sell

### Quick Research Add-Ons per Prospect:
- [ ] Check clinic LinkedIn for volunteer coordinator
- [ ] Search clinic website for "careers" or "jobs" page (volunteer system may be there)
- [ ] Call main number, ask for volunteer coordinator
- [ ] Check if they mention Volgistics/Vsys anywhere (web, social, job postings)
