# ClinicalHours — Pitch Deck

**Tagline:** Find. Apply. Track.
**Positioning:** The future of medical applications.

## The Problem

**For Students:** 150,000 pre-med students compete for clinical hours each year with zero standard process.
- **No Discovery** — students search one by one
- **No Common App** — email, phone, walk-ins; each handled manually
- **No Logging** — hours and accomplishments go untracked

**For Hospitals:** 1,500+ emails. One coordinator. Peak season.
- **Email Flood** — coordinators handle hundreds of unfiltered applications with no tools
- **No Standard Process** — every channel handled manually

## Our Solution

One platform. Two sides. Zero friction.

| For Students | For Clinics |
|---|---|
| Find 9,000+ clinics | Minimize email chaos |
| Apply directly | Filter applications |
| Track & log hours | Schedule interviews |
