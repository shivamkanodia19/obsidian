## Questions Sorted by Topic

### Topic: Kinematics & Coordinate Systems

**Polar Coordinate Kinematics (Nearly always appears)**

| Exam | Problem | Focus | Difficulty | Points |
|------|---------|-------|-----------|--------|
| [2007] 1a | Derive $\hat{r}$ and $\hat{\theta}$ components | Derivation | Easy-Medium | 20 |
| [2007] 1b | Find force in polar motion | Application | Medium | 5 |
| [2008] 1 | Same as 2007 1a | Derivation | Easy-Medium | 25 |
| [2008] 4b | KE in polar coordinates | Kinematics | Easy-Medium | 6 |

**Key Strategy:** Memorize the standard forms:
- $v_r = \dot{r}$, $v_\theta = r\dot{\theta}$
- $a_r = \ddot{r} - r\dot{\theta}^2$, $a_\theta = r\ddot{\theta} + 2\dot{r}\dot{\theta}$

| Exam | Problem | Context | Difficulty | Points |
|------|---------|---------|-----------|--------|
| [2007] 2b | Ball bounces off object | Setup only | Easy-Medium | 12 |

**Key Strategy:**
- Inelastic: $p_i = p_f$, energy lost
- Elastic: $p_i = p_f$ AND $KE_i = KE_f$
- Always use momentum conservation first

### Topic: Angular Momentum & Conservation

**Angular Momentum Conservation (Very common—~25% of exam)**

| Exam | Problem | Scenario | Difficulty | Points |
|------|---------|----------|-----------|--------|
| [2007] 3b | Arms pull in (decreasing I) | Figure skater | Easy-Medium | 8 |
| [2007] 3c | Arms pull in + man has mass | Composite system | Medium | 8 |
| [2008] 3a | Mass moves toward axis | Variable moment of inertia | Hard | 8 |
| [2008] 3c | Same as 3a but rod has I | Composite system | Medium-Hard | 8 |

**Key Insight:** When no external torques, $L = I\omega = constant$
- If I decreases → ω increases
- If I increases → ω decreases

**Standard Problem Pattern:**
1. Calculate initial $L_i = I_i \omega_i$
2. Calculate final $L_f = I_f \omega_f$
3. Equate: $I_i \omega_i = I_f \omega_f$
4. Solve for unknown
