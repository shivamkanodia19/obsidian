## MASTER PATTERN: All 15 Exams

**Format (100% consistent):**
- 4 problems per exam
- 25 points each (100 total)
- 50 minutes

**Problem 1: ALWAYS Polar Coordinates (25 pts, 100% frequency)**
- Identical across all 15 years
- Derive velocity components: $v_r = \dot{r}$, $v_\theta = r\dot{\theta}$
- Derive acceleration components: $a_r = \ddot{r} - r\dot{\theta}^2$, $a_\theta = r\ddot{\theta} + 2\dot{r}\dot{\theta}$
- Always worth 25 points guaranteed

**Problems 2-4: Variable by year, consistent topics**
