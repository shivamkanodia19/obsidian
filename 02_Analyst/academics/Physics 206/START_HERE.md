### Strategy for Test Day

**First 5 min:** Skim all problems, identify easy/medium/hard

**Next 40 min:** 
- Solve easy problems first (quick confidence)
- Move to medium (bread & butter points)
- Attempt hard if time (partial credit possible)

**Last 5 min:** Quick check for obvious errors

**Key:** Show all work even if unsure (partial credit)
