## Sending Checklist

Before you send:

- [ ] **Verify emails** (check against `verified-contacts-all-20.md`)
- [ ] **Personalize [Name]** in greeting (e.g., Pete DeSoto, not [Pete/Matt])
- [ ] **Phone number** in signature (add your actual number, not XXX-XXXX)
- [ ] **Send timing:** Tuesday–Thursday, 9am–11am or 2pm–4pm (higher open rates)
- [ ] **Track opens** (use Boomerang for Gmail, or manual tracking in a spreadsheet)
- [ ] **Follow-up cadence:**
  - Day 3 (no open): Re-send with minor subject line variation
  - Day 7 (no reply): Send LinkedIn message (see backup versions)
  - Day 10: Phone call (if number available)

## Email Success Indicators

**You're doing it right if:**
- ✅ Open rate 20%+ (at least 4 of 20 open the email)
- ✅ Reply rate 2–5% (at least 1 person replies)
- ✅ Meeting booking rate 30%+ of replies (1–2 calls booked from 20 emails)

**If open rate is <15%, adjust:**
- Subject line is not compelling enough (test variations)
- Sending time is off (try 9am instead of 2pm)
- Email domain reputation (warm up domain if needed)
