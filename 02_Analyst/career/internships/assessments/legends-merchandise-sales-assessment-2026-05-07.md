## Durable Findings From The Completed Package

Based on [[05_Outputs/research-analysis/legends-merch-assessment-2026-05-07/Executive_Summary]]:

- 2024 in-stadium merchandise revenue rose modestly to `$2.774M`, up `0.5%` from `$2.761M` in 2023.
- Growth came more from higher transaction volume than larger baskets, since transactions rose while sales per transaction slipped.
- `T-shirts` showed the strongest year-over-year lift, while `Jerseys` remained the dominant revenue anchor.
- `Lower Concourse` was the clearest location-level growth driver, while `Club Level`, `Outfield`, and `Upper Deck` lagged.
- The strongest operational recommendations were to rebalance assortment toward winning categories and zones, recover basket size with bundles, and clean the data model before the next season.
