**Method:** Tier-based sending with problem validation checkpoints.

## Tier 2: Validate Then Send (Medium Confidence — Inference Based)

**Confidence Threshold:** 60–80% (Indirect signals; company structure or positioning language suggest pain, but no direct employee/company evidence)

**Validation Approach:** Before sending, do 10 min of targeted research to confirm problem is real.

---

   - Employee mentions of hiring pace

4. **LinkedIn decision-maker posts:** Check William A. Beitler or Mike Shaver's LinkedIn for recent posts on:
   - Growth announcements (signals expansion = hiring pressure)

**Validation Result Options:**

**✅ IF confirmed:** Proceed to send. Problem is validated.

**❌ IF contradicted:** Don't send this angle. Pivot to different positioning (e.g., "automation to reduce manual ops" instead of "hiring")

### Email 5: BSP — Founder/Operations

**Problem:** Warehouse + trucking staff separately, but demand overlaps = coordination gap + labor waste

**Current Confidence:** 50% (Inferred from company structure; NO evidence of actual pain)

**Validation Research (Do This First):**

1. **LinkedIn search:** Find founder/owner of BSP Warehousing
   - Search: "BSP Warehousing Dallas" + "founder" OR "owner" OR "CEO"
   - Look at their profile for operations/growth signals

2. **Company website:** Check for:
   - How they market warehouse vs. trucking (separate services?)
   - Any language about "integrated" or "unified" operations (if they don't mention it, they may not be thinking about it)

3. **LinkedIn DM to founder (Pre-email Qualification):**
   - "Hi [Name], quick question: Does BSP handle warehouse and trucking scheduling together or separately? Asking because I think there might be coordination opportunities. Open to a 10-min call?"
   - **Purpose:** Confirm they have the problem before sending cold email
   - **If yes:** They acknowledge the problem. Send the email.
   - **If no:** They say it's not a pain. Don't send this angle.
   - **If no response:** Assume lower priority; hold off on this email for now.

**Validation Result Options:**

**✅ IF confirmed via DM:** Send the cold email. Problem is now validated.

**❓ IF DM gets no response:** Don't send cold email. Move BSP to "defer" list.

**❌ IF contradicted:** They say it's not an issue. Don't send.

**Timeline:**
- LinkedIn DM: Apr 16 (wait 24–48 hours for response)
- Research: 5 min
- Send (if confirmed): Apr 18

## Key Principle: No Assumptions

**Every email sent must have:**
- ✅ Problem verified OR validated before sending
- ✅ Company-specific detail (not generic)
- ✅ Decision-maker confirmed
- ✅ Contact info verified

**Never send an email where you're assuming the problem.** If you're <70% confident, do validation research first.
