## Email Selection Guide

**Use EMAIL 1 (Enrollment Forecasting) if:**
- Superintendent's main pain point is avoiding reactive school closures
- District wants to plan facility needs + staffing proactively
- You want to lead with predictive planning (higher value positioning)

**Use EMAIL 2 (Budget Optimization) if:**
- Budget crisis is the most urgent problem
- You want to frame enrollment forecasting as cost-containment solution
- CFO/budget committee is equally influential as superintendent

**Recommended Approach:** Send EMAIL 1 first (proactive planning resonates with educational leaders). If no reply by Day 7, follow up with EMAIL 2 (reframes as budget solution).
