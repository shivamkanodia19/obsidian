## Workflow Applied to All Emails

**Tier-Based Sending (from sending-workflow.md):**
- **Tier 1:** ≥90% confidence; verified problems; send immediately
- **Tier 2:** 60-80% confidence; validate before sending (10 min research + LinkedIn DM for qualification)
- **Tier 3:** <60% confidence; don't send

**Problem Validation Checklist (before every send):**
- [ ] What is the problem?
- [ ] What is the evidence?
- [ ] Is the evidence direct or inferred?
- [ ] Which Tier does this fall into?
- [ ] Have I done validation research (if Tier 2)?
- [ ] Am I assuming anything?
- [ ] Would the decision-maker recognize this problem as real?

**Email Standards (Vault Frameworks Applied):**
- ✅ Subject: "I can [action] your [problem] [outcome] ([timeframe])"
- ✅ CTA: Question-based ("Can you...?"), not command
- ✅ 55-65 words (no fluff)
- ✅ No self-focus (no "I'm TAMU", no "looking for internship")
- ✅ Lead with THEIR problem (not my research)
- ✅ Specificity = credibility (always include numbers)
- ✅ Constraint → Mechanism → Outcome structure
- ✅ No em dashes; direct phone number in signature
