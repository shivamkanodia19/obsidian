## SEARCH TIPS

1. **Start with company name + title.** Example: `"Lewisville ISD" superintendent`
2. **Use quotes for exact phrases.** Helps filter results.
3. **If exact search fails, try broader search.** Example: `lewisville school district superintendent`
4. **Check company LinkedIn page.** Click on company, view "Employees" tab, search for title.
5. **Verify the person is current.** Check LinkedIn title says "currently works at" (not "worked at").
6. **Copy LinkedIn profile URL** (example: `linkedin.com/in/johndoe/`) for reference.
