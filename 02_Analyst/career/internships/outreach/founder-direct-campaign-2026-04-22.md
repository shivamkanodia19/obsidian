# Founder-Direct Internship Outreach - April 22 Campaign

## Current Decision

The outreach strategy shifted from smaller local operational targets toward larger founder/CEO-direct targets, using the successful Homebase pattern as proof. The strongest working template is:

1. freshman at Texas A&M studying Industrial & Systems Engineering
2. build AI-powered operations and workflow automation tools
3. ClinicalHours proof: deployed across multiple clinic partners and cut admin overhead by 70%
4. company-specific operating hook
5. quick 15-minute fit call

## Contact Quality Rule

Do not draft to guessed addresses. A target is usable only when the email or route is public and source-backed, or when the company has an official routing inbox. Stale contacts are explicitly held back in each wave file. This was especially important for acquired or leadership-changed companies such as HUVRdata, Ambyint, data.world, and Slingshot Aerospace, and for larger platforms until a clean official route surfaced.
