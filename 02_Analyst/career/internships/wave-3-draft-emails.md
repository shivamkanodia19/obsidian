## Notes for Refinement

**Tone:** Direct problem statement → concrete 10-week solution → simple CTA. No fluff.

**Length:** Each email is 120–150 words (scans in <1 min, shows research depth).

**Pain point specificity:** Every email leads with verified, documented operational issue (not generic).

**Internship framing:** Position as "I could build X this summer" not "I'm looking for an internship where I learn about X."

**CTA:** Simple one-liner (not aggressive follow-up instruction).
