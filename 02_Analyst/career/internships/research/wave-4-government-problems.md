**Problem 1: Catastrophic Bond Project Timeline Delays**

- Major city infrastructure (streets, parks, public facilities) stalled for 5-7+ years
- Root cause: Scope creep, design delays, procurement bottlenecks, internal coordination failures

- Status: Zero facilities started; zero facilities completed (entire $20M program has zero progress after multiple years)
