## Research Execution Workflow

For each district (1-5):

1. **TEA Database Lookup (5 min per district):**
   - Visit https://tea.texas.gov/
   - Search district by name
   - Find: Enrollment data (current + 5-year trend), financial reports, accountability ratings
   - Document: Current enrollment, enrollment change %, budget status

2. **News Search (5 min per district):**
   - Google: "[District Name]" + "enrollment decline" OR "superintendent" OR "state takeover"
   - Look for: Recent news about budget, closures, leadership changes
   - Document: Key headlines + findings

3. **District Website Financial Reports (5 min per district):**
   - Visit district website → Finance/Budget section
   - Look for: Annual financial reports, bond referendums, budget shortfalls
   - Document: Budget deficit amount (if any), bond amounts, facility issues

4. **Glassdoor Teacher Review Search (5 min per district):**
   - Glassdoor: Search "[District Name] School District"
   - Look for: "enrollment pressure", "overcrowded", "understaffed", "budget cuts", "closures"
   - If 3+ reviews mention same problem = strong confidence
   - Document: 2-3 representative quotes

5. **LinkedIn Superintendent Search (5 min per district):**
   - LinkedIn: Search "[District Name]" + "Superintendent"
   - Record: Superintendent name, LinkedIn URL
   - Note: Tenure (new vs. established), background

6. **District Contact Info (2 min per district):**
   - District website: Find superintendent contact or main office
   - Record: Phone, email (if available), mailing address

**Total per district:** 25 minutes  
**Total for 5 districts:** ~2 hours
