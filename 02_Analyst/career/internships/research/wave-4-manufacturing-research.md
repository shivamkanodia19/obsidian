**Goal:** Deep-dive Glassdoor + LinkedIn research on 3-5 DFW manufacturers (50-200 employee range) to verify equipment downtime + production efficiency problems. Find Tier 1 confidence evidence.

**Problem Angles to Look For:**
- Equipment downtime / unreliable machines / breakdown culture
- Production delays / missed deadlines / rushing + rework

- Overtime culture + burnout language

## Identified Problems (For Reference)

**Equipment Downtime:**
- Reactive maintenance (fix when broken, not preventively)
- Old equipment with frequent failures
- Lead times on replacement parts = extended downtime
- Solution positioning: "Predictive maintenance forecasting"

**Production Delays:**
- No visibility into bottlenecks
- Rushes at end of quarter
- Rework due to quality failures
- Solution positioning: "Production timeline forecasting"

**Labor Inefficiency:**
- Can't forecast demand surges
- Overtime culture + high turnover
- Solution positioning: "Production demand forecasting → labor scheduling"
