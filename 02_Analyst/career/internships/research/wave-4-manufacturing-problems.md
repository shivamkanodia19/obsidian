# Wave 4 Manufacturing Subset — Real Problems Research

**Goal:** Identify real, leverage-able operational problems from DFW manufacturers (50-200 employee range). Then run Phase 2 qualification (decision-maker, contact info, real/active).

**Methodology:** Using [[02_Analyst/career/internships/strategy/industry-frameworks#INDUSTRY 2 MANUFACTURING]] positioning: "Reduce production downtime + optimize equipment reliability"

---

## Manufacturing Industry Pain Points (General)

DFW manufacturing sector has 4,043+ companies. Common problems in 50-200 employee manufacturers:

1. **Equipment Downtime** — Reactive maintenance instead of predictive; unplanned downtime costs 5-10% of production capacity
2. **Labor Scheduling Inefficiency** — Cannot forecast demand surges; overtime + understaffing both occur
3. **Supply Chain Disruptions** — Lead time variability; cannot forecast component needs; inventory imbalance
4. **Quality Control Issues** — Reactive inspection instead of predictive; rework costs high
