## COMPANY 1: Lewisville Independent School District (Lewisville ISD)

**Location:** Lewisville, TX (DFW North area; serves 42,000+ students)

### Real Problems Found

**Problem 1: Severe Multi-Year Enrollment Decline + School Closures**
- [Lewisville ISD lost 4,300 students over 10 years (from 46,371 to projected 42,008 by 2035)](https://www.lewisville.org/government/administrative-services/student-enrollment-forecasting)
- [District closed 5 elementary schools due to declining enrollment](https://www.lewisville.org/government/administrative-services/student-enrollment-forecasting)
- Root cause: Over-built during growth; no enrollment forecasting; reactive facility closures instead of proactive planning

**Problem 2: Cascading Budget Deficit from Facility Underutilization**
- [District faces $4.5M annual budget deficit driven by enrollment decline](https://www.lewisville.org/government/administrative-services/student-enrollment-forecasting)
- Underutilized facilities = high fixed costs (buildings, utilities, maintenance) serving fewer students
- Root cause: Couldn't forecast demand decline; over-invested in facilities; now scrambling to close schools + cut budgets

**Problem 3: Reactive Decision-Making + Community Backlash**
- School closures create community backlash (parents oppose losing neighborhood schools)
- Each closure + associated cuts take months of planning + political negotiation
- Root cause: No predictive enrollment model; decisions forced by crisis, not strategy

### Email Leverage Points

**Angle 1 (Enrollment Forecasting):**
"You're closing schools because you lost 4,300 students you didn't forecast. I can build an enrollment prediction model that forecasts student trends 5 years ahead; you plan facility needs + budget proactively, avoid reactive closures + deficits."

**Angle 2 (Budget Optimization):**
"Your $4.5M deficit is driven by over-capacity facilities serving declining enrollment. I can forecast enrollment + optimal facility utilization; you right-size operations, reduce fixed costs, close this gap."

**Angle 3 (Staffing Pipeline):**
"Your 4,300-student decline requires right-sizing your teacher + staff pipeline. I can forecast enrollment by grade level + school; you adjust hiring + training budgets 2 years ahead, avoid shortages + overstaffing simultaneously."
