**Goal:** Extract real, leverage-able operational problems from DFW nursing homes with verified CMS deficiencies. Then run Phase 2 qualification (decision-maker, contact info, real/active).

## COMPANY 1: DFW Nursing & Rehab — Fort Worth

**Location:** Fort Worth, TX (98 beds)

### Real Problems Found

**Problem 1: CMS Health Deficiencies + Regulatory Fines**
- [7 health deficiencies reported to CMS](https://projects.propublica.org/nursing-homes/homes/h-455881)
- [$87,446.25 in penalties/fines assessed](https://projects.propublica.org/nursing-homes/homes/h-455881)
- Deficiency areas likely include: staffing adequacy, care coordination, infection control, documentation
- Root cause: Reactive compliance; no systematic auditing/forecasting of audit risks

**Problem 2: Severe Staff Turnover + Retention Crisis**
- [57.4% nursing staff turnover rate](https://projects.propublica.org/nursing-homes/homes/h-455881) (industry avg: 30-40%)
- [60% RN turnover rate](https://projects.propublica.org/nursing-homes/homes/h-455881) (industry avg: 20-25%)
- Root cause: Likely compliance stress causing burnout; staff leave due to unsafe conditions + regulatory pressure

**Problem 3: Interconnected Staffing-Compliance Spiral**
- High turnover → Loss of experienced staff → Increased compliance violations
- Compliance violations → Regulatory scrutiny → Staff stress → Increased turnover
- Breaking cycle requires: proactive staffing forecasting + compliance risk visibility

### Email Leverage Points

**Angle 1 (Compliance):**
"You have 7 CMS deficiencies + $87K in fines. Root cause: reactive auditing. I can build a compliance risk model that flags likely violations 8 weeks ahead using your facility data; you fix proactively, not reactively."

**Angle 2 (Staffing):**
"Your 57% nursing turnover is destroying morale and creating new compliance violations. I can forecast staffing needs 8 weeks ahead so you maintain consistent RN coverage; staff retention improves + compliance violations decrease."

**Angle 3 (Combined):**
"Your staffing crisis (57% RN turnover) is causing your compliance violations (7 CMS deficiencies, $87K fines). I can build an integrated staffing + compliance model: predict staffing needs + flag compliance risks together. 8-week pilot."
