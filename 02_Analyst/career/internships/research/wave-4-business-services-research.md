# Wave 4 Business Services Deep Research — Staffing + Credentialing Cycle Time

**Goal:** Deep-dive Glassdoor + LinkedIn research on 2-3 DFW staffing/credentialing firms to verify cycle time + process efficiency problems. Find Tier 1 confidence evidence.

**Problem Angles to Look For:**
- Long credentialing/background check turnaround times
- Bottlenecks in placement pipeline (candidate → placed → onboarded)
- Client complaints about slow hiring process
- Candidate complaints about unclear timelines
- Understaffing during peak hiring seasons
- Manual process inefficiency (paper-based, no automation)

## Identified Problems (For Reference)

**Credentialing Cycle Time:**
- Manual verification processes (paper-based, phone calls)
- Regulatory delays (medical board clearances, background checks)
- No systematic forecasting of candidate readiness
- Solution positioning: "Credentialing timeline forecasting"

**Placement Pipeline Bottlenecks:**
- Candidate → screened → interviewed → placed → onboarded
- No visibility into which step is slow
- Clients waiting weeks for placements
- Solution positioning: "Placement pipeline forecasting → bottleneck identification"

**Capacity Constraints:**
- Can't scale staffing operations to handle demand surges
- Peak hiring seasons overwhelm team
- Turnaround times slip during busy periods
- Solution positioning: "Staffing demand forecasting → team capacity planning"
