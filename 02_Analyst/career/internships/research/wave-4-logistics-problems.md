## COMPANY 1: WSI (Warehouse Specialists, Inc.) — Dallas

**Location:** Dallas, TX (4 DFW locations; 282K+ sq ft)

### Real Problems Found

**Problem 1: Understaffing + Overtime Culture**
- [Glassdoor reviews show "often seem to be understaffed at times"](https://www.glassdoor.com/Reviews/Warehouse-Specialists-Reviews-E390239.htm)
- [Floor employees work 50+ hours/week regularly; management works 55+ hours/week](https://www.glassdoor.com/Reviews/Warehouse-Specialists-Reviews-E390239.htm)
- Root cause: No demand forecasting; staffing reacts to surges instead of predicting them

**Problem 2: Manpower Gap**
- [Employees report "need for more manpower for the amount of work"](https://www.glassdoor.com/Reviews/Warehouse-Specialists-Reviews-E390239.htm)
- Indicates either under-hiring OR inability to forecast seasonal peaks (e.g., holiday peaks, client surges)

### Email Leverage Point
"Your teams are working 50+ hours/week because you're staffing based on current demand, not next month's. I can build a forecasting model that predicts surges 4 weeks ahead, so you schedule teams right-sized to actual demand and cut overtime costs."
