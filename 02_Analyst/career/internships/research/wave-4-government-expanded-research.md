## Research Execution Workflow

For each city (1-5):

1. **Municipal Bond Database (5 min per city):**
   - Search: Municipal Securities Rulemaking Board (MSRB) EMMA database (emma.msrb.org)
   - Search city name + bond projects
   - Find: Bond amount, issue date, current status
   - Document: Total capital program value, active bonds

2. **News Search — Project Delays (5 min per city):**
   - Google: "[City Name]" + "bond" + "delay" OR "project delay" OR "overdue"
   - Look for: Recent articles about bond projects slipping, procurement issues, scope creep
   - Document: Specific projects delayed, timeline extensions, dollar impact

3. **City Council Meeting Minutes (5 min per city):**
   - Visit city website → City Council → Meeting Minutes
   - Search minutes for: "capital projects", "bond", "procurement", "delays"
   - Look for: Discussions of project challenges, timeline extensions, staffing issues
   - Document: Key discussion points + decisions

4. **News Search — Procurement Issues (5 min per city):**
   - Google: "[City Name]" + "procurement" + "delay" OR "bottleneck"
   - Look for: Contractor performance issues, design delays, permitting bottlenecks
   - Document: Specific procurement challenges

5. **LinkedIn City Manager Search (5 min per city):**
   - LinkedIn: Search "[City Name]" + "City Manager"
   - Record: City manager name, LinkedIn URL, tenure
   - Note: Whether established or new manager

6. **City Contact Info (2 min per city):**
   - City website: City Manager's office contact
   - Record: Phone, email, mailing address

**Total per city:** 25 minutes  
**Total for 5 cities:** ~2 hours
