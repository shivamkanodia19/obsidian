## Personalization Depth (To Be Validated)

### Level 1: Generic (Current)
`Hi [Name], I'd love to learn how [Clinic Name] currently manages volunteers...`

### Level 2: Medium (Size-Based)
`Hi [Name], managing [X] volunteers with email must be chaos...`
*(Researched volunteer count beforehand)*

### Level 3: Deep (Research-Based)
`Hi [Name], saw that [Clinic Name] is [recent hiring/news]. As you scale volunteers, [pain point] becomes critical...`
*(Researched recent activity, specific pain point)*

### Level 4: Ultra-Personalized (Relationship)
`Hi [Name], [mutual connection] mentioned your volunteer program. I'd love to learn...`
*(Found mutual connection via LinkedIn)*

**Hypothesis:** Level 3 > Level 2 > Level 1, but effort increases significantly. Optimal balance likely at Level 2-3.

## CTA Variants (To Be A/B Tested)

### Variant 1: Time-Specific
`Would you be open to a 15-minute call Tuesday or Wednesday next week?`
*(More specific = higher response)*

### Variant 2: Low-Friction
`Would a 10-minute call work for you?`
*(Shorter commitment = higher agreement)*

### Variant 3: Offer Value First
`I'm compiling a report on volunteer management best practices at free clinics. Would you be open to a quick chat to share your approach?`
*(Positions as peer conversation, not sales pitch)*

### Variant 4: Demonstration-Focused
`I'd love to show you how [Clinic Name] is using ClinicalHours. Would a 15-min demo work?`
*(More specific than generic "chat")*

## Nonprofit Cold Email Benchmarks (To Be Validated)

**Industry averages (estimated):**
- Open rate: 20-30% (lower than B2B, nonprofits are busy)
- Reply rate: 3-5% (lower engagement, limited email checking)
- Meeting rate: 20-40% of replies (nonprofit decision-making slower)

**Goals for ClinicalHours:**
- Open rate: 25%+ (niche + personalization)
- Reply rate: 5-10% (targeted + problem-focused)
- Meeting rate: 30%+ (compelling offer + strong CTA)

## Email Delivery & Technical

- **Domain:** clinicalhours.org (good for deliverability)
- **Sending cadence:** 5-10 per day (avoid spam filter)
- **Email validation:** Validate addresses before sending
- **Tracking:** Open/click tracking (but disclose in privacy policy)
- **Warm-up:** Start with 2-3 per day, ramp to 10/day over 1 week
