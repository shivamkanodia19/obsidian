# HVAC Intake QA

This hub stores the strongest saved version of the local-service "AI mystery shopper" idea after pressure-testing it.

## Start Here

- [[02_Analyst/projects/hvac-intake-qa/hvac-intake-qa-mvp-2026-05-29]] - durable memo with the verdict, wedge, MVP shape, and build sequence

## Core Takeaway

- The real opportunity is not "AI calls random businesses." It is "find intake failures, prove them quickly, and use that to sell deeper audits or fixes."
- HVAC is the best first vertical because the phone matters, the scenarios are simple, and the compliance burden is lower than healthcare or legal.
- A single call is best used as a teaser for cold outreach, not as enough evidence for a full unsolicited audit.

## If This Gets Revived

- Start with one metro, one HVAC scenario, one short call, one structured findings schema, and human-reviewed outbound email.
- If the cold wedge gets replies, the real service can expand into a paid 3-5 call audit and eventually opt-in monthly intake QA.
