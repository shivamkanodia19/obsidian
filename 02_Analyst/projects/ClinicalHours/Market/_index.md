## Key Takeaways

### Market
- **DFW TAM:** 15-20 free clinics with 50+ volunteers (our target segment)
- **Pricing power:** $1,200/year is sustainable (clinic directors value time savings)
- **Competitive landscape:** Free clinics are ignored by large platforms; uncontested niche

### Go-to-Market
- **Positioning:** "Purpose-built for clinics, affordable, built by someone who runs one"
- **Price advantage:** 2-4x cheaper than Volgistics/VSys; better ROI than DIY hidden labor cost
- **Proof:** BCS FHC pilot clinic is live

### Key Risks to Validate
- Pricing sensitivity (can free clinics actually commit to $1.2K/year?)
- HIPAA compliance costs (might block adoption or enable premium pricing)
- Expansion beyond DFW (is the model regional or scalable nationally?)
