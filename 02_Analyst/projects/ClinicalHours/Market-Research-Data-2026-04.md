# Clinical Hours Market Data — Clinic Inventory

## Overview

Ascension-affiliated outpatient clinic network data (338K CSV). Contains facility names, locations, contact info, facility types. Focused on Ascension Michigan market (Grand Blanc region).

**Source:** [[01_Source/projects/ClinicalHours/Asc Clnicals - Sheet1 (1).csv]]

## Key Fields

- **Facility_Name** — Clinic name + specialization
- **Ministry_Market / Ministry** — Parent org + ministry code (MIGRA, etc.)
- **Address_1, Address_2, City, State, Zip_Code** — Full location data
- **Phone_Number** — Direct clinic contact
- **County** — Geographic clustering
- **Facility_Type** — All marked "Outpatient Clinic" (relevant: no inpatient services = volunteer-friendly)
- **Business_Entity** — "Ascension Enterprise" (large health system = budget avail + formal processes)
- **Facility_Status** — "ACTIVE" (operational, not closed/pending)

## Strategic Insights

### Market Characteristics

1. **Ascension Focus**: Large integrated health system = established volunteer/intern programs likely already in place. BUT: outpatient-only clinics may lack formal infrastructure (no hospital HR + volunteer coord).

2. **Specialization Opportunity**: Data shows clinics by specialty (Neurosciences, Cancer, Women & Children). Each has distinct volunteer needs:
   - **Neurosciences** — higher skill req, longer commitment
   - **Cancer** — compassion + stability critical
   - **Women & Children** — family-friendly, high turnover

3. **Geographic Clustering**: Grand Blanc region (Michigan) = confined territory. Good for pilot + relationship building.

4. **Volume**: 338K clinics but filtered to Ascension + Michigan = smaller pilot set. Validate product-market fit here before scaling nationally.

### Go-to-Market Application

**Who to target first:**
- Outpatient clinics with specializations known for volunteer turnover (W&C, primary care)
- Secondary: established clinics with existing volunteer programs (more sophisticated demand, better reference)

**How to position:**
- Not replacing their volunteer programs, augmenting them (remove admin burden via ClinicalHours matching + hours tracking)
- Appeal: compliance + scheduling (clinics already have processes; we reduce overhead)

**Why this data matters:**
- Validates existence of target market (not hypothetical)
- Shows specialization diversity (messaging angle for each)
- Large org affiliation = longer sales cycle but sticky contracts
