## Strategy Summary

**Approach:** Problem-first personalization
- Show specific understanding of operational pain (not generic product pitch)
- Propose concrete Week 1 deliverable (not vague "help")
- Reference ClinicalHours proof (you built exactly what they need)
- Position as operator, not vendor

**Why it works:**
- Clinic directors make decisions based on operational pain + ROI
- ClinicalHours founder credibility is differentiator
- Manual processes are entrenched (high switching cost post-implementation)
- Price is favorable vs. DIY (time savings) and competitors (2-4x cheaper)

**Execution:**
- Wave 1: 4 Tier 1 clinics (50+ volunteers, clear pain signals)
- Wave 2: 5 Tier 2 clinics + Austin expansion
- Wave 3: Houston + San Antonio metro

**Success metrics:**
- 15-20% email response rate
- 1-2 demos scheduled from Wave 1
- 1 customer signed by end of April
