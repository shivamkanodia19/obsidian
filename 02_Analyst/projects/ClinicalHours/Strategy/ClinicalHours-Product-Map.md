# ClinicalHours Product Map

This note maps the current product scope so strategy and outreach notes can point to one durable reference instead of implying a broader platform than the evidence supports.

## Current Validated Workflow

- Pilot evidence is strongest around evaluation follow-up and adjacent admin coordination.
- The safest current claim is workflow relief for small clinics, not a fully proven operations platform.
- Reference: [[02_Analyst/projects/ClinicalHours/clinicalhours-experiment-log-framing-2026-05-07]]

## Near-Term Product Wedges

1. Evaluation automation
2. Onboarding reminders
3. Status tracking across admin steps

These wedges all sit inside the same pain cluster: staff juggling email, forms, Drive, and manual follow-up.

## Ideal Customer Shape

- Small clinics with 15-100 volunteers
- Manual or fragmented volunteer/admin workflows
- Budget sensitivity that makes enterprise tools feel too heavy

Reference: [[02_Analyst/projects/ClinicalHours/Strategy/niche-refinement]]

## Longer-Term Direction

- Broader small-clinic operations support
- Deeper workflow coordination across volunteer, onboarding, and evaluation steps
- Potential larger-clinic or hospital tier only after stronger small-clinic proof
