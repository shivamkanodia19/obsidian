# ClinicalHours Clinic Target Outreach (April 2026)

## Outreach Strategy: Problem-First Personalization

Adapted from Summer 2026 internship cold email framework. Each clinic target includes:
- **Personalization hook** (specific pain signal)
- **Concrete Week 1 deliverable** (not vague product pitch)
- **Email angle** (why NOW, why this clinic)

## Email Framework (Under 120 words)

**Subject Line Options:**
- "[Founder name] — volunteer coordination solution built for clinics like yours"
- "Quick idea for [Clinic name]'s volunteer scheduling"
- "Reducing [Clinic name]'s volunteer admin burden"

**Body Structure:**
1. **Problem Recognition** (1-2 sentences): Acknowledge their volunteer scale + pain
2. **Specific Offer** (1 sentence): What gets done in Week 1
3. **Proof** (1-2 sentences): ClinicalHours + specific feature relevant to them
4. **The Ask** (1 sentence): 15-minute call to see if it fits
5. **Sign-off**: Shivam Kanodia / clinicalhours.org | 214-470-0598

**Email Word Count Target**: Under 120 words, no em dashes

---

## Example Email (Mercy Clinic Friends)

**Subject:** Mercy Clinic Friends — volunteer coordination solution

Hi [Director name],

I looked at your volunteer program (140+ active volunteers is impressive). Managing that at scale with email + spreadsheets must be consuming 20+ hours/month from someone.

I built ClinicalHours—volunteer management platform for clinics. Our pilot clinic went from manual scheduling chaos to 30-second digital check-ins.

In 5 days, I can migrate your current intake process to a digital form that auto-sorts applicants by clinic role. No switching costs—just better data.

Can we schedule 15 minutes this week to see if it fits your workflow?
