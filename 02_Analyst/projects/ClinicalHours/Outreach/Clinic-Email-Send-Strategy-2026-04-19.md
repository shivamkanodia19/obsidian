## Response Classification System

### Positive
- Expresses interest ("This looks interesting", "Tell me more")
- Requests demo or call
- Asks specific questions about features
- **Action:** Schedule 15-min call immediately

### Neutral
- Acknowledges receipt ("Thanks for sharing")
- "Will review and get back to you"
- No strong signal either way
- **Action:** Phone call follow-up (48 hours); warm, conversational

### Negative
- "Not interested"
- "We use [competitor]"
- "No time for this"
- **Action:** Archive (no follow-up unless they re-engage)

## Phone Escalation Procedure

**When:** No response to 2 emails (Day 7-10)  
**How:**
1. Call clinic main line; ask for volunteer coordinator
2. Keep pitch to 60 seconds: name, what you do, why you're calling
3. Listen for pain points; don't pitch features
4. Ask: "Would 15 minutes next week work to show you how this could help?"
5. If hesitant: "No worries—just checking in. Open to it later?"
6. Log outcome (accepted call, rejected, promised to email back, etc.)
