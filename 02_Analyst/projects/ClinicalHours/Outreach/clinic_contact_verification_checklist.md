## Quick Lookup Tool

**To find emails quickly from Gmail:**

1. Open Gmail
2. Search: `from:@[clinic-domain] to:me`
   - Example: `from:@mercyclinicfriends.org to:me`
3. Find most recent email from clinic
4. Click email to see full headers
5. Copy sender email address

**Alternative:** Search Gmail for clinic name: `[Clinic Name] volunteer coordinator`

## Bounce Handling Procedure

**If email bounces after send:**

1. **Soft bounce** (temporary): Retry in 3 days
2. **Hard bounce** (permanent): Remove from list, find alternative contact
3. **Unknown bounce**: Try once more, then escalate to phone
