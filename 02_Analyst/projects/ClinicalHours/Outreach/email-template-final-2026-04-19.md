## Why This Works

1. **Opening is warm** — "Hi!" sets conversational tone without being unprofessional
2. **Introduction is complete** — Engineering student + co-founder position established immediately
3. **Value prop is specific** — "Replace the email-and-spreadsheet mess" is concrete, not generic
4. **Social proof is concrete** — BCS Free Health Clinic in Bryan (specific, local, real)
5. **Geographic relevance** — Mentions Brazos Valley expansion (shows thinking about their region)
6. **Research signal** — Mentions clinic's community (shows you did homework)
7. **Discovery question** — "Learn how your team manages" (consultative, not salesy)
8. **CTA is warm but clear** — 15-minute call, specific ask
9. **Signature is complete** — URL, phone, name
