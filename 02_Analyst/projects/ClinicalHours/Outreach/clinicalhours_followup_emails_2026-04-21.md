# ClinicalHours Follow-Up Email Batch (2026-04-21)

**Purpose:** Second-touch outreach for clinics where first email was successfully delivered  
**Timing:** Send 3-5 days after original outreach (if no response received)  
**Strategy:** Reference previous email subtly, add new value (case study, testimonial, specific feature update), lower response barrier  
**Success Target:** 20-30% response rate on follow-ups (vs 15-20% on cold outreach)

---

## Follow-Up Email Format

**Subject:** [Contextual variation from first email]  
**Body Strategy:**
- Line 1: "Following up on my earlier message..."
- Line 2: New value angle (BCS Free Health Clinic case study, testimonial from similar clinic, new feature)
- Line 3: Lower-friction CTA ("Just checking in..." or "Wanted to add one thing...")
- CTA: Same meeting request but with updated timing options
