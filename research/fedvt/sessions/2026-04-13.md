# FEDVT Paper — 2026-04-13

## What We Worked On

Conducted comprehensive PhD-level critique of FEDVT paper (introduction v3, Section 2 draft, working paper, literature index) from perspective of agricultural economics and animal science specialist. Benchmarked against American Journal of Agricultural Economics (AJAE) standards and identified structural gaps, reference verification issues, and validation requirements for journal submission.

## Decisions Made

- **Prioritize validation as critical gap**: Backtesting break-even calculations against Kansas FOF historical data (2015–2023) is required before journal submission. No validation reported yet.
- **Emphasize novel contribution**: Position FEDVT as integration of 65-parameter cost accounting + live CME data + interactive visualization — distinct from Flores (individual-animal level), Stika (placement optimization), and Herrington (DSS adoption gap).
- **Section 3 & 4 are structural essentials**: Methods and Results sections must be drafted before journal submission; cannot proceed with background/intro alone.
- **Clarify tool name**: Use "**Feedlot Economic Decision and Valuation Tool**" consistently (not "Visualization"). Valuation terminology better captures break-even / enterprise valuation logic.

  - VBA computational engine architecture (object model, workbook organization)
  - Tableau integration approach (static exports vs. live connection, performance)
  - Formal validation methodology description
  - Figures 2–3 (architecture diagram, dashboard screenshots)

- **Section 4 (Results)** not drafted — needs:
  - 4–6 scenario simulations (base case, high feed cost, disease, futures prediction)
  - Comparative table vs. traditional enterprise budget methods
  - Sensitivity analysis heatmap (Figures 4–5)

- **No historical validation yet** — break-even calculations not backtested against Kansas FOF 2015–2023 closeout data

- **No user testing conducted** — adoption depends on practitioner feedback (per Bang, Laugen & Dreyer 2023)

- **Reference verification needed:**
  - Coffey et al. (2018) DOI correction (currently incorrect Cambridge prefix)
  - Stika (2025) — clarify if published in journal or thesis-only
  - USDA (2025) Cattle on Feed — specify exact report date/month before submission
  - Blakebrough-Hall figures in AUD, not USD — need USD conversion in Section 2
