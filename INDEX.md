# 🧠 Shivam's Second Brain

Master navigation for your vault. **Source** is truth. **Analyst** is synthesis.

---

## 📁 Vault Structure

**`/01_Source/`** — Your raw writing, analysis, dumps
- Never edited by Claude
- Organized by topic as you upload
- Your source of truth

**`/02_Analyst/`** — Your persona in synthesis
- Built from Source files via `/save`
- Evolves as you write more
- Linked back to Source via wikilinks

**`/04_Archive/`** — Historical content
- Old Analyst History sections (>6 months)
- Conflict archives
- Managed automatically

---

## 🚀 Quick Start

### You uploading new content?
1. Add text to `/01_Source/[Project]/[Topic]/filename.md`
2. Call `/save`
3. Claude organizes Source, synthesizes Analyst

### You resuming work?
1. Call `/resume [topic]`
2. Claude loads your Analyst persona + relevant Source files
3. You have full context

### You need to check on something?
1. Read `/02_Analyst/_index.md` to find relevant files
2. Explore from there
