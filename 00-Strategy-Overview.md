# Content Strategy Overview

## Core Positioning

**ClinicalHours unlocks clinical experience for pre-med students who don't know where to start.**

### Customer Problem Arc
1. **Awareness:** "I need clinical hours but don't know where to find them"
2. **Consideration:** "What actually counts? How do I apply? Will this help my med school app?"
3. **Decision:** "This clinic/platform solves my problem"
4. **Action:** "Sign up, find hours, track progress"

---

## Content Pillars (4)

### Pillar 1: #ClinicMindset (Motivation & Authority)
**Goal:** Establish ClinicalHours as the authority on clinical careers + personal growth  
**Why:** Pre-med students are motivated by goals (med school, PA school, etc.). Lead with inspiration + education.

### Pillar 2: #HoursHack (Practical Solutions)
**Goal:** Drive awareness of ClinicalHours platform through actionable, immediate value  
**Why:** Students want quick answers: "Where do I start? What's the easiest clinic to get into?"

### Pillar 3: #CredBuild (Application Strategy)
**Goal:** Position ClinicalHours as essential for med school/PA applications  
**Why:** Direct tie to student outcomes. "We help you build the app-winning experience."

### Pillar 4: #CareerPath (Exploration)
**Goal:** Educate about specialties, career options, shadowing, etc.  
**Why:** Helps students figure out their path → more engaged, longer lifetime value.

---

## Why the 7-Day Rotation System?

✅ **Predictable:** Students know what to expect each day (builds habit)  
✅ **Batches easily:** Film/write 7-8 videos/posts once per week, schedule daily  
✅ **Diverse:** Addresses all 4 pain points without feeling repetitive  
✅ **Flexible:** Easy to swap dates for events (keep the 7-theme structure, adjust timing)  
✅ **Data-driven:** Weekly theme data = clear insights into what resonates

## Distribution Strategy

### Platform Differences

**TikTok:** Trending sounds, fast-paced, personality-forward voiceover  
**Instagram Reels:** More polished, captions on text, educational tone  

**Approach:** Same content, slight format tweaks for each platform
- TikTok: Use trending audio + hooks in first 3 seconds
- Instagram: Cleaner design, captions enabled, CTA sticker

### Posting Cadence
- **Faceless slideshows:** 1/day across both platforms (post same video to both)
- **Speaking videos:** 2-3/week, can cross-post to YouTube/LinkedIn too

---

## Success Metrics

**Leading Indicators (measure weekly):**
- Engagement rate (comments, saves, shares per post)
- Follower growth (+100-200/week is healthy at this stage)
- Video completion rate (% who watch to end)
- CTR to platform (tracked via link in bio)

**Lagging Indicators (measure monthly):**
- New signups from social
- LTV of social-acquired students
- Cost per acquisition (if spending on ads)

**Growth Targets:**
- Week 1-4: Build to 5-10K followers, test which themes resonate
- Week 5-12: Scale best performers, optimize posting times
- Month 4+: Stabilize at daily cadence, expand into YouTube/newsletter
