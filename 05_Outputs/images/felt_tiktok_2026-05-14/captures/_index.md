# Felt TikTok Captures

Real product captures and supporting metadata used as source material for the slideshow.

## Sections

- `home/` - home feed and return-surface screenshots
- `lobby/` - lobby, invite, and host-state captures
- `guided/` - guided table, live play, and feedback captures
- `training/` - trainer and practice captures
- `excluded/error-states/` - unusable or misleading states kept for reference
- `review/duplicates/` - duplicate captures kept out of active selection
- `figma/` - Figma import JSON files and Figma-state notes
