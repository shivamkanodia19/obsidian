## Email Tips

✅ **DO:**
- Keep tone curious, not salesy
- Lead with their problem, not your solution
- Mention internship context
- Use specific numbers from research
- Keep it under 100 words
- End with a question ("Would you be open...?")

❌ **DON'T:**
- Say "I can solve this" (too confident)
- Demand their time ("Call Wed/Thu")
- Mention being a student without context ("I'm a TAMU junior...")
- Use em dashes (use semicolons instead)
- Make unverified claims
