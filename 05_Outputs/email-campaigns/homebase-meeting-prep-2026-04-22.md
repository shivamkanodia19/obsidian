   Build a lightweight internal view that classifies missed breaks, late punches, missing clock-outs, and payroll-risk issues so support or product teams can see the highest-friction patterns.

2. Customer onboarding activation  
   Map the steps from signup to first schedule, first clock-in, first payroll export, or first team message. Build a dashboard that flags where small businesses stall.

3. Support-routing analytics  
   Use support-ticket exports or CRM data to cluster repeat issues by product area, customer segment, and urgency, then create a simple routing or prioritization workflow.

4. Churn / re-engagement signals  
   Identify accounts with declining usage before they churn, using signals like fewer shifts, missed payroll setup, low employee activation, or reduced manager logins.

5. Small AI assistant prototype  
   Prototype one internal helper that summarizes account status or suggests next actions for support, onboarding, or customer success teams.
