# Direct LinkedIn Outreach

Date: 2026-04-21
Goal: reach actual operators first. Use direct email only when public and specific. Use LinkedIn when the direct email is not public.

## Response Model

Use this sequence:

1. View profile.
2. If they posted recently, leave one real comment or like first.
3. Send a short connection note. No resume dump. No generic internship ask.
4. After acceptance, send one concise message: their constraint, your ClinicalHours proof, one 8-week prototype, one 10-minute ask.
5. Follow up after 7 days. LinkedIn says most InMail responses arrive within a week, and Friday/Saturday underperform.

Why this works:
- LinkedIn data says shorter InMails tend to get better response rates.
- LinkedIn recommends individual, personalized messages over bulk sends.
- LinkedIn reports single personalized InMails can lift response rates by 30%.
- Best hook for you: "I built ClinicalHours workflows across 200+ users. I can prototype the same visibility layer for your operating bottleneck."
