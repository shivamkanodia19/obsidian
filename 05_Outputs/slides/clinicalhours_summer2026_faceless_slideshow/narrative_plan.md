# ClinicalHours Summer 2026 Faceless Slideshow Narrative Plan

## Audience

Students trying to line up clinical hours for Summer 2026 without bouncing between tabs, school docs, random clinic pages, and scattered notes.

## Objective

Create a faceless student-facing slideshow that turns Summer 2026 timing into a clean hook while staying inside the current truthful ClinicalHours student proof lane.

## Narrative Arc

1. Summer timing hook plus scattered-search problem
2. Map-based discovery
3. Faster clinic preview
4. One-account follow-through for saved clinics and hours
5. Calm low-friction start

## Framework Rules

- Stay inside `student_discovery_secondary`.
- Use proof assets first, then write copy against them.
- One dominant proof asset per slide.
- Keep headlines short, student-facing, and non-meta.
- Let the proof get more specific each swipe.
- Treat `Summer 2026` as a timing hook, not as a promise of outcomes.

## Visual System

- Portrait `1080 x 1920` faceless slideshow slides.
- Bright editorial field with soft blue, mint, peach, and sand glow shapes.
- Large upper-left headline, one short sentence, and real ClinicalHours proof below.
- White rounded screenshot frames, dark ink typography, and small colored chips for pacing.
- No human faces, no fabricated UI, and no decorative proof that outruns the visible asset.
